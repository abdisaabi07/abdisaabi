History

Ethiopian athletics has a long and storied history dating back to ancient times. The country is known for producing some of the world's greatest long-distance runners, including Haile Gebrselassie and Kenenisa Bekele.

In recent years, Ethiopia has also become a force in middle-distance and sprint events. The country's success can be attributed to a number of factors, including a high-altitude training environment and a culture that values athleticism and hard work.
